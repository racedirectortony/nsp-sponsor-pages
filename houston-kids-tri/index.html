The event

Houston's biggest family morning.

Every spring, more than 1,600 kids swim, bike, and run across two days in Katy: the largest kids triathlon in the world. Their families turn out in force, making it the biggest gathering of Houston families Negative Split Productions produces all year.

For a sponsor, the kids are the event but the parents are the audience. These are household decision-makers with real spending power in exactly the categories that fit a family festival: finance, healthcare, food, insurance, and family services. The kids compete. The families show up, linger for hours, and spend.

When
May 22 & 23, 2027 (two days), 7:00 AM starts.
Where
Typhoon Texas Waterpark in Katy, TX. The swim leg is in the waterpark.
Format
Swim, bike, and run for ages 6 to 17: short course, long course, Splash & Dash, plus paratriathlon and adaptive divisions.
The draw
More than 1,600 young athletes across two days, and an estimated ~9,000+ on site once you count parents, siblings, and grandparents.

Who shows up

A loyal, affluent West Houston family audience.

Kids Tri draws Houston-metro families with kids aged 6 to 17, concentrated in affluent West Houston (Katy, Sugar Land, the Fort Bend corridor). Parents are the real audience: engaged, health-minded, higher-income households making purchases for the whole family. Over half the field comes back every year, the strongest loyalty of any Negative Split Productions event.

About the producer

Negative Split Productions was founded in 2014 with the goal of producing premier endurance events in the greater Houston area. Over the years, NSP has created events ranging from road race series to extreme triathlons in remote destinations around the world, as well as the world's largest kids' triathlon, hosted annually in Katy.

Partner with us and you are working with the team that produces and times the event start to finish, so your activation is handled with care and your brand shows up right. For more information visit mynegativesplit.com.

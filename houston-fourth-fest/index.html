The event

Houston's Fourth of July party.

Every Independence Day, Houston Fourth Fest turns Vintage Park into a patriotic street festival: a road race (5K, 9K, 17.76K, and a Family 1.776K) and Houston's Beer Mile Championship. It is the loudest, most social morning on the NSP calendar.

For a sponsor, that is the opportunity. This is a young-adult, experience-seeking crowd that comes to celebrate, not just to race, and it is the fastest-growing event in the portfolio: up 40% in two years. Great for on-site activation and sampling, not just a logo on a banner.

When
Every July 4th (Independence Day), morning through early afternoon.
Where
Vintage Park, northwest Houston (the Champions / Vintage Park district).
Format
Road Race (5K, 9K, 17.76K, and a Family 1.776K), plus Houston's Beer Mile Championship.
The draw
1,200+ participants in 2026 and climbing fast (up 40% in two years), with an estimated ~4,500 on site once you count the festival crowd.

Who shows up

The youngest, fastest-growing crowd we have.

Fourth Fest draws a young-adult crowd (median age 33 and dropping), the most balanced gender mix of the three events, spread across greater Houston with the widest reach and a north/northwest lean. The Beer Mile gives it a party-and-festival identity that pulls groups, teams, and spectators, not just runners.

About the producer

Negative Split Productions was founded in 2014 with the goal of producing premier endurance events in the greater Houston area. Over the years, NSP has created events ranging from road race series to extreme triathlons in remote destinations around the world, as well as the world's largest kids' triathlon, hosted annually in Katy.

Partner with us and you are working with the team that produces and times the event start to finish, so your activation is handled with care and your brand shows up right. For more information visit mynegativesplit.com.

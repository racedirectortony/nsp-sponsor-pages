The event

Houston's New Year's Day run.

Since 2023, the Houston Resolution Run has been the city's first finish line of the year. Every January 1, runners and walkers gather at Fish Plaza in the heart of downtown to start the year in motion, before the confetti is even swept up. It has grown into a real Houston tradition: a fresh-start ritual for people who set goals and mean it.

For a sponsor, that timing is the whole opportunity. This is the single morning of the year when your audience is most open to a new gym, a new supplement, a new bank, a new pair of shoes. Resolution Run puts your brand in front of them at the exact moment they are deciding who to become this year. It is a smaller, tighter field than a big-city marathon, and that is the point: a concentrated, loyal, local crowd you can actually reach.

When
Friday, January 1, 2027, 9:00 AM start. A yearly tradition since 2023.

Fish Plaza, 501 Texas Ave, in the heart of downtown Houston.

~1,100 participants, and an estimated ~2,750 on-site once you count family and spectators. Growth driven by a new social media program that lifted our other events in 2026.

Who runs it

A loyal, hyper-local Houston audience.

Resolution Run draws a female-leaning, 30s-to-40s runner base at the peak goal-setting moment of the year. These are highly motivated, disciplined people with discretionary income to spend on their health and their goals, and they are making those buying decisions right now. They train, they invest in gear, they join gyms, and they show up on January 1 to prove it. It is the most concentrated and most local audience of the three Negative Split Productions owned events: not a travel race, a come-back-every-year one.

About the producer

Negative Split Productions was founded in 2014 with the goal of producing premier endurance events in the greater Houston area. Over the years, NSP has created events ranging from road race series to extreme triathlons in remote destinations around the world, as well as the world's largest kids' triathlon, hosted annually in Katy.

Partner with us and you are working with the team that produces and times the event start to finish, so your activation is handled with care and your brand shows up right. For more information visit mynegativesplit.com.
